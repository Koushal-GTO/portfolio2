Hey there! I'm Koushal, a passionate web developer from the beautiful city of Belbari in Nepal. At 18, I'm still figuring out many things, but one thing I'm certain about is my love for creating things with code.

My journey into programming started during high school when I was trying to build a simple website for our school project. What began as a necessity quickly became an obsession. I spend most of my free time either coding, reading about new technologies, or diving into amazing web novels like Shadow Slave and Lord of the Mysteries.

I believe in learning by doing, so most of my skills come from experimenting with different projects and occasionally breaking things (a lot). This portfolio itself is my attempt to showcase what I've learned so far, with all its imperfections and rookie mistakes.
